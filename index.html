About Summit

The Manipal Entrepreneurship Summit (MES) 2025 is scheduled from February 6th to 8th, 2025. MES 2025 will bring together students, faculty, innovators, entrepreneurs, and investors, offering a platform for innovation, networking, and collaboration. Join us for Innovation Mela 2025, a key highlight of the Manipal Entrepreneurship Summit (MES) 2025, which will be on February 8th, 2025, from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. The last date for registration is 15th January 2025.
